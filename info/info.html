what is Covid-19

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2).

Origin of Covid-19

The disease was first identified in December of 2019 in Wuhan, the capital of China's Hubei province, and has since spread globally, resulting in the ongoing 2019–20 coronavirus pandemic.

Transmission

it is primarily spread during close contact and by small droplets produced when people cough, sneeze or talk with close contact being within 1–3 m.
